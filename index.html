Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY-G)
Ministry of Rural Development

Description: Provides financial assistance to BPL families to construct pucca houses with basic utility systems.

Benefits: ₹1.20 lakh for plain areas, ₹1.30 lakh for hilly/NE/LWE areas. Plus MGNREGS support for construction labour.

Eligibility: Families without pucca houses, BPL category, Aadhaar linked bank accounts required.

PM Ujjwala Yojana 2.0
Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

Description: Free LPG connection with first refill and gas stove provided to eligible BPL families.

Benefits: Free LPG connection + 1 free refill cylinder + free hot plate stove unit.

Eligibility: Women aged 18+, BPL/SECC families, no existing family LPG connection.

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Description: Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to all farmer families in 3 installments of ₹2,000 each.

Benefits: ₹2,000 every 4 months directly transferred to bank account.

Eligibility: Small & marginal farmers with cultivable land. Aadhaar and bank account mandatory.
